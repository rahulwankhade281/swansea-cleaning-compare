Money-saving tips

How to get a cheaper cleaning quote

Small choices at booking time can save you a surprising amount every year.

📅

Book mid-week

Monday to Wednesday slots are in lower demand. Many Swansea cleaners offer up to 15% off mid-week bookings.

🔁

Go regular, not one-off

A fortnightly or monthly contract almost always costs less per visit than a one-off clean. Ask about recurring rates.

📦

Bundle your services

Need carpet cleaning and a domestic clean? Booking both with one company usually unlocks a bundled discount.

🔑

Offer key access

Allowing your cleaner to hold a key removes the need for you to be home, which can lower your hourly rate by £2–4.

📍

Stay local

Cleaners charge for travel time. Choosing a company based in your area (Sketty, Uplands, Mumbles) keeps costs down.

⭐

Compare at least 3 quotes

Our data shows Swansea residents who compare 3+ quotes save an average of £28 per clean vs. booking the first result.
